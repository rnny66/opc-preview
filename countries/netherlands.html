Poker in the Netherlands

The Netherlands has a vibrant poker community with tournaments held regularly across the country. OPC partners with local organizers to bring international recognition to Dutch poker players.

Whether you play in Amsterdam, Rotterdam, or anywhere else in the Netherlands, your results count towards the European rankings.
