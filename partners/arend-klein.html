About

Poker Arend

Poker Arend is a Dutch entertainment company specializing in professional poker equipment and tournament experiences. Based in Hekelingen, the Netherlands, they supply high-quality poker tables imported directly from manufacturers — cutting out the middleman to offer professional-grade equipment at competitive prices.

Beyond equipment, Poker Arend organizes weekly live poker tournaments through their Arend Poker Series and provides corporate team-building experiences that bring the excitement of tournament poker to businesses across the Netherlands.

As official equipment and events partner of OPC Europe, Poker Arend supplies the tables and tournament infrastructure that make our events possible. Their hands-on experience running live poker series ensures that every OPC tournament delivers a professional, authentic playing experience.
