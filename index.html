What is OPC?

OPC connects live poker tournaments across Europe into one international ranking system.

Players compete locally and earn points towards a European championship.

All events are verified, all players are age-checked. It's completely free for players and organizers.

Why join OPC?

FOR PLAYERS

Play. Compete. Get Ranked

  • Find tournaments across Europe
  • Get ranked internationally

Our Founders

Founded by Poker Legends

Marcel Lüske, the founder of OPC, is a professional poker player with over $5 million in career tournament earnings and 20+ years of experience. European Player of the Year (2001, 2002), ranked #1 in Europe, and founder of the International Poker Rules (FIDPA). Now bringing transparency, fairness, and international standards to European amateur poker through OPC.

"The Flying Dutchman"

Noah Boeken

Noah Boeken is a legendary Dutch professional poker player who rose to fame as a Magic: The Gathering European Champion before becoming the first Dutchman to win an EPT title in 2005. A protégé of Marcel Lüske, he has amassed over $2.9 million in live tournament earnings, including a major victory at the Master Classics of Poker.
